Partner With Us!

Join ANGO Competitive in Building Community Through Sports

We're a student-led sports organization based in Richmond Hill, dedicated to bringing athletes, families, and fans together through fun, competitive, and community-driven experiences. As we continue to grow, we're looking to partner with local businesses that share our commitment to community.

Our goal is to highlight businesses that support local sports while giving them visibility with our players and spectators. Whether you're a general company looking to connect with a young, active audience, or a sports company wanting to strengthen community ties, we have partnership opportunities that can work for you.

How We Feature Our Partners

Marketing & Promotion

Your logo on flyers, posters, and social media promotions with business shout-outs during tournament announcements.
